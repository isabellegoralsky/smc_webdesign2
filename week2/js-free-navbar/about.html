About the Sulcata Tortoise

The African spurred tortoise, also called the sulcata tortoise, is a species of tortoise inhabiting the southern edge of the Sahara desert in Africa. It is the third-largest species of tortoise in the world and the largest species of mainland tortoise. Sulcatas live up to 80 years old, and take 15-20 years to reach their full size!

Sulcatas are known to be very sociable with their owners. They can form relationships with their owner, recognize them and have unique personalities.
